About us

Amobit technologies is an ICT company in Uganda with Talented ICT specialists with a lot of skills aiming to solve community problems using new technology. Organizations face challenges on their digital evolution journey. New technologies offer new possibilities for where and how to conduct business.Customers expect more as competitors raise the bar on what constitutes good service. Amobit technologies services deliver solutions that meet the challenges.We’re a network of innovators. We devise bold solutions to the most client challenges. It’s our ability to explore new technologies to design best-fit solutions

Mission

To develop and sell reliable and sustainable Digital Solutions.

Vision

Avail a Robust interconnected software ecosystem for our community.

Our Core Values

Customer Centricity

Our products, services and all our operation focus most on satisfying and tackling customer needs. We always develop software that solve pressing matters of the people in our community who we take as our first hand customers.

Integrity

We understand and take seriously the matter of privacy and personal data protection therefore, we in the first place take it as our priority to safe guard all the personal information from our customers through our products.

Innovation

Without creativity in solving problems then we are doing contrary to our mission therefore we must always consider innovative solutions to our problems more so in the ever rapid changing technology world.

Boldness

We endeavor to take fast steps towards identified opportunities because its only in this way we can make a positive impact in our community and ensure our continuous survival.

Team Work

We value the philosophy of working together to achieve a common goal. In this we believe we can deliver quality products and services faster.

Responsibility

AMOBIT TECHNOLOGIES will always work and act in responsible ways not to harm societies for this is our valuable market of today and tomorrow. Harming them is harming ourselves.
